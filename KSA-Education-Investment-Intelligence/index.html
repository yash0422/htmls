What this tool does

This tool maps where education demand is across Saudi Arabia — by expat nationality, population density, and curriculum preference — and cross-references it against existing school supply to surface investment gaps. It ends with a live financial model where you configure a school on any plot and get a full BTL P&L in seconds.
