The DPS Group operates as an independent, UAE-registered trademark ("Delhi Private School LLC") — distinct from India's Delhi Public School Society, whose franchise was intentionally not renewed in 2017. The Group's MD, Dr. Dinesh C. Kothari, has built a multi-campus educational enterprise across all four UAE schools under a single private ownership structure. This gives the group unilateral expansion authority — no society approval required for new geographies.

Group Milestones
JAN 2000
DPS Sharjah founded by Abha Sehgal. 146 students. First DPS-brand school in UAE under franchise from DPS Society, India.
FEB 2002 / 2003
DPS Dubai established in Jebel Ali — second school under same group. Begins co-educational model from Grade 1.
2005
Launch of CLP (Comprehensive Learning Program) — early digital education infrastructure for student-parent engagement.
2014
Khalifa Award for Education awarded. WWF Green Flag certification earned. DPS Dubai becomes fully co-educational.
MARCH 2017
India franchise agreement allowed to lapse. DPS LLC (Kothari) proceeds independently under UAE-registered trademark. Strategic independence secured.
NOV 2019
Trademark victory — UAE court orders DPS Ajman to rename. DPS LLC confirmed sole rights to "Delhi Private School" brand in UAE.
APRIL 2021
DPS Ras Al Khaimah opens — 3rd school. Brings 21 years of SHJ/DXB operational heritage to a new emirate.
2025–26
DPS RAK rated "Distinguished Private School" by HH Ruler of RAK. DPS Sharjah wins Green Schools award again (2026). Group at ~17,000+ students.
